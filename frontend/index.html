2. Getting Started

To make a trade, you need to sign upto 2 transactions.

  1. Invoke the From Contract's approve to approve the To Contract an allowance (an amount of your choosing) for transfer, if you haven't already approved.
  2. Invoke the To Contract's buy with the correct amount of From stablecoins and To stablecoins to make the trade.

4. Design

4.1 Rate Sourcing

Source: https://data.chain.link/ethereum/mainnet/fiat

The system currently sources FX rates from Chainlink oracles. This implies there is significant difference in 1) the frequncy the rates update and 2) the deviation when compared to real-world FX rates. In particular, most pairs only update rate when there is 0.15%+ rate change or 24 hours after last price update. One of the reasons being lowering gas cost.

4.2 Commission

0.3% for all pairs per trade.
