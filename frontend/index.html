Getting Started

To make a trade, you need to sign upto 2 transactions.

  1. Invoke the From Contract's approve to approve the To Contract an allowance (an amount of your choosing) for transfer, if you haven't already approved.
  2. Invoke the To Contract's buy with the correct amount of From stablecoins and To stablecoins to make the trade.
